Boreal Toad Species Distribution Model

The aim of the study was to use multiple environmental variables such as temperature, precipitation and land cover to predict and describe habitat suitability and distribution of the Boreal Toad. This was carried out using species distribution modeling (SDM) which determines the relationships between locational occurrences of a species and the environmental attributes of that site.

The model shows that habitat preference and distribution to be best described by climatic variables, as temperature and precipitation were the highest contributors to the final model.
